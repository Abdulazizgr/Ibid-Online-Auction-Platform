About iBid Company

iBid Company is a licensed Ethiopian-based auction company specializing in online auctions of all types. We were established by a highly accredited team of licensed auctioneers and technologists with extensive experience in local and international marketing trends. Our firm is a full-service auction company with specialists in every facet of the auction industry in Ethiopia and international marketing.

We bring an automated strategy to maximize value through our secured platform and handle a wide range of current and future auction needs, in items or services marketing. We are dedicated to providing a secure and reliable auction management process for our clients (individuals or enterprises) from listing contract signing to successful sale.


Our Services

Our extensive range of services includes asset tracing, inventory listing, uplifting of any movable assets, transportation and warehousing, professional valuations, and disposals, whether by private treaty or specialized auctions. We continuously exceed expectations by utilizing expertise to unlock maximum value for our clients.

Marketing items cycled through our auction framework include Home Auctions, Machinery Auctions, Office and Bank Buildings, Electronics, Automobiles, Commercial and Residential Real Estate, Fine Art, License Plates, and more. We provide fully automated auction services to convert merchandise into cash and turn auctions into worldwide events.
